What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies to manage customers, sales, and business operations.

What is CRM?
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) helps companies store customer details and improve communication with customers.

Why Companies Use Salesforce?
Manage customer data
Track sales
Improve customer service
Automate business processes

Salesforce Objects
Account
Represents a company or organization.

Contact
Represents a person related to the company.

Opportunity
Represents a potential business deal or sale.


Business Flow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

Real-World Example – College Admission System
Account = College

Contact = Student

Lead = New Applicant

Opportunity = Admission Process
